Artificial Intelligence, or AI, refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines.
These machines are designed to think, learn, and make decisions like humans.
AI is used in a variety of fields such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and education.

Key Applications:

  • Natural Language Processing – Enables chatbots and voice assistants like Siri and Alexa.
  • Computer Vision – Powers facial recognition, self-driving cars, and medical imaging.
  • Machine Learning – Allows systems to learn from data and improve over time.

Famous Quote:
"The science of making machines smart — that's the essence of AI."

AI began as an academic discipline in 1956, and has since grown rapidly.
Early AI models were rule-based, but now use traditional programming deep learning and neural networks.
